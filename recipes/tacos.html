tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe carne asada
  • 2 avocados
  • 3 tbsp cotija cheese
  • 1/3 cup onion (finely diced)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • tortillas

Steps

  1. grill the steak
  2. slather tortilla with avacodo
  3. add a good helping of chopped carne asada
  4. sprinkle of cotija or queso fresco cheese
  5. chopped onion and fresh cilantro
  6. Squeeze some fresh lime on top
